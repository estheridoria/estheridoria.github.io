Potato Salad (vegetarian) Easy Fluffy Quick Bread (1.5-2 hours before eating it) 
        1) Mix in bottom of breadmaker and let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy:
          2.75t yeast
          0.5t sugar
          0.25c warm water
        2) Add in order:
          0.75c + 2T warm water
          3T butter/vegetable oil
          3T 0.5t sugar
          1.5t salt
          3c bread flour
        3) Make sure the bread flour completely covers the wet ingredients like a blanket

        4) Click "Rapid"/"Quick" on the bread machine and let run.
          Optional: after the mixing stops, you can re-center the bread in the pan so it comes out more evenly at the end

        4) After the cook timer goes to 0, pull the bread out and let cool on a rack 15-20 minutes before eating
